Our Philosophy

Coffee with a conscience.

YellowBean was built on a single belief — that great coffee should never be ordinary. We source ethically from smallholder farms in Ethiopia, Colombia and Sumatra, roast in small batches, and pull every shot with the same patience our growers pour into their harvest.

  • Direct-trade beans from 14 verified farms
  • Roasted in-house, never older than 7 days
  • Compostable cups, lids and pastry sleeves
  • Baristas trained for 90+ hours before the bar

What we serve

More than just caffeine.

Four reasons regulars keep coming back — and one reason they bring their laptops.

Artisanal Roasts

Small-batch beans roasted on a 1958 Probat. Light, medium and dark profiles, dialed weekly.

Co-Working Wi-Fi

Gigabit fiber, unlimited refills on filter coffee, and power outlets at every seat. Yes, every seat.

Fresh Pastries

Croissants laminated at 4am, sourdough by 7. Vegan, gluten-free and gloriously buttery options daily.

Private Events

Cupping sessions, latte-art classes, book launches and after-hours bookings for up to 80 guests.
